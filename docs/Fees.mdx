---
title: Fees
---

Fees on the Open Dollar platform are transparent and predictable with no inconvenient changes or surprises.

Collected $OD from fees revenue partly funds the $ODG-governed DAO treasury and is also used to buy and burn $ODG tokens

Stability fee

Borrowing $OD is subject to an interest fee of 2% APR.

Liquidation Penalty

The liquidation penalty depends on the vault type, but is generally it's around 20%.
